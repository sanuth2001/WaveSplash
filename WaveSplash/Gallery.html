ART AND CRAFT

Craft Fairs and Events: Craft fairs and events celebrate the craftsmanship and creativity of artisans and crafters. These events provide a platform for artists to showcase and sell their handmade products, such as pottery, textiles, jewelry, and woodwork. Craft fairs offer a unique opportunity for visitors to discover unique and one-of-a-kind pieces while supporting local artists and artisans in their craft journeys.

Art and Design Festivals: Art and design festivals bring together artists, designers, and enthusiasts from various disciplines to celebrate creativity and innovation. These festivals often include workshops, talks, installations, and interactive experiences that engage participants in the artistic process.

Performing Arts Shows: Performing arts, including theater, dance, music, and opera performances, are a significant part of art and craft entertainment. These shows provide captivating experiences that blend artistic expression, storytelling, and emotional resonance, leaving audiences moved and inspired.

Art Fairs and Marketplaces: Art fairs and marketplaces create opportunities for artists to connect with potential buyers and collectors. These events are essential for artists to gain exposure, expand their networks, and showcase their artwork to a wider audience.

DINE IN MOVIES

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema: Alamo Drafthouse Cinema is a well-known chain of dine-in theaters that originated in Austin, Texas. They are renowned for their commitment to providing a high-quality movie experience with a full-service kitchen and bar. Guests can enjoy a variety of gourmet dishes, drinks, and snacks while watching the latest films on the big screen

Cinépolis Luxury Cinemas at Hollywood Park: Cinépolis is set to open a new dine-in luxury cinema in Inglewood, California, as part of the Hollywood Park development. This 55,000-square-foot multiplex will offer full waiter service to patrons, including its IMAX theater. It will be one of the world's only dine-in IMAX theaters, providing an immersive movie

AMC Dine-In Theatres: AMC Dine-In Theatres is another popular chain that offers dine-in movie experiences. They provide a wide selection of fresh and hand-crafted dishes, allowing guests to enjoy restaurant-quality cuisine while watching their favorite movies. In addition to dining options, AMC Dine-In Theatres also offer premium formats like RealD® 3D, Dolby Cinema®, and IMAX® technologies, providing a superior audio and visual experience

Dine-in movie theaters have gained popularity for their unique and immersive movie-watching experiences. They offer a great way to enjoy a night out, combining the joy of cinema with the pleasure of dining, making it a memorable and enjoyable experience for movie enthusiasts and food lovers alike

VIDEO GAMES

Handheld Gaming Devices and VR: 2023 saw the release of several handheld gaming devices, providing competition to the successful launch of Valve's Steam Deck in the previous year. VR also received continued support, with the release of PSVR 2 in February and Meta Quest 3 later in the year

Video Game Conventions: As the COVID-19 situation improved and restrictions eased, many in-person video game conventions were resurrected in 2023. Events like GDC, Quakecon, BlizzCon, and others made a comeback, bringing together gaming communities and enthusiasts. However, E3 was permanently retired during this time

The Super Mario Bros Movie: In April of 2023, the Super Mario Bros Movie was released and became the first film based on a video game franchise to gross over $1 billion at the box office, marking a significant milestone for video game adaptations in the film industry

Upcoming Games in 2023: 2023 is set to bring an extensive list of new video game releases, covering a wide range of genres and platforms. Some of the highly anticipated titles include Starfield, Final Fantasy 16, Marvel’s Spider-Man, Assassin’s Creed, and more. Players can expect a mix of new IPs and the return of beloved franchises such as Diablo and Pikmin

WATER PARK

Great Wolf Lodge chain has opened its latest indoor waterpark resort in Perryville, Maryland. This waterpark resort offers not only a huge indoor waterpark but also other attractions such as MagiQuest, a mirror maze, bowling, a ropes course, and mini-golf, ensuring a fun-filled experience for guests of all ages.

The Island Waterpark, located at the Showboat Resort in Atlantic City, New Jersey, is a 120,000-square-foot indoor waterpark. It features 11 water slides, a large lazy river, and a unique glass retractable roof by OpenAire, allowing visitors to enjoy the waterpark experience year-round. Additionally, there is an exclusive adult-only pool and a swim-up bar for adults looking for a more relaxing experience away from kids.

Vikingløp is a new addition to the Rulantica waterpark at the Europa-Park resort in Rust, Germany. It is an enclosed eight-lane matt-racing slide with large high-speed spirals and a layout that doubles back upon itself, providing an exhilarating experience with impressive theming and lighting effects.

The Wilderness Resort in Wisconsin Dells has added two new racing slides: Warped Wagons, where guests race down riding in tubes, and Twisted Trails, a thrilling two-lane matt racer slide. Both slides feature the latest "translucent" lighting effects and provide exciting competition for guests.
